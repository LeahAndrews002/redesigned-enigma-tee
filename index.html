Email Security Awareness

Protect yourself and your organization from email threats

Why Email Security Matters

Email is one of the most common ways attackers gain access to systems. A single click on a malicious link can lead to:

  • Data breaches
  • Financial loss
  • Identity theft
  • Unauthorized system access

Common Email Threats

  • Phishing: Fake emails that trick you into giving away sensitive information.
  • Malware: Attachments or links that install harmful software.
  • Spoofing: Emails that appear to come from trusted sources.

How to Stay Safe

  • Check the sender’s email address carefully
  • Do not click suspicious links
  • Avoid downloading unknown attachments
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
  • Report suspicious emails immediately

Spot a Phishing Email

  • Urgent or threatening language
  • Requests for passwords or sensitive info
  • Unusual links or attachments
  • Misspellings and poor grammar

Test Your Awareness

Think before you click. Would you open a suspicious email?
